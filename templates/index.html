How to get your Twitter cookies:
  1. Open x.com in your browser and make sure you're logged in
  2. Press F12 (or right-click → Inspect)
  3. Go to the Application tab (Chrome/Edge) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  4. Click Cookies → x.com in the left menu
  5. Find auth_token and ct0 — double-click their Value cells, press Ctrl+C
  6. Paste them into the fields above and click Save Cookies

Needs to be refreshed about once a week. The bot falls back to Nitter automatically if cookies expire.
