How to get your Twitter cookies:
  1. Open twitter.com or x.com and log in
  2. Press F12 to open DevTools
  3. Go to Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  4. Click Cookies on the left, then x.com
  5. Copy the Value fields for auth_token and ct0
  6. Paste them above and click Save Cookies
